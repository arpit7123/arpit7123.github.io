About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science at Arizona State University. My area of research are Natural Language Understanding, Semantic Parsing, Commonsense Reasoning, Commonsense Knowledge Extraction and Automated Question Answering.

I graduated with a Master's degree (summa cum laude) in Computer Science from Arizona State University in December 2014. During my master's degree I worked in the field of Natural Language Understanding, on the topic of hard Co-reference resolution and Semantic Parsing. My thesis is titled, "Solving Winograd Schema Challenge : Using Semantic Parsing, Automatic Knowledge Acquisition and Logical Reasoning".

I worked as an Application Support Engineer Intern at The Mathworks Inc. from May 20th 2013 to August 16th 2013 at the Natick, MA, USA headquarters.

Prior to that i have a working experience of 1.6 years in an India based multinational IT firm named Infosys Technologies.

I graduated with a bachelor's degree (Bachelor in Technology) in Computer Science with honors in the year 2010 from India.
